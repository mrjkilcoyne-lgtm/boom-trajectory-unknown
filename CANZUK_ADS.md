## Notes for the designer

- Mocks 01, 05, 08 want a single dominant typeface — slab serif (01), Johnston-derivative (05), heavy geometric sans (08).
- Mock 02 wants the most whitespace your medium allows.
- Mock 03 wants neon-on-black; the em-dashes carry the rhythm.
- Mock 04 reads as a real shell; ship it as a CLI capture for technical channels.
- Mock 06 is for long-form scroll surfaces — newsletter, blog header, deep-link landing.
- Mock 07 is recruitment-shaped; it goes on the careers page and on stickers handed out at conferences.

All seven are deliberately CANZUK-flagged. The strategic argument is the product. Don't soften it.
